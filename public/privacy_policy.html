Privacy Policy

ProcessSpy macOS App Privacy Policy

The ProcessSpy macOS app respects your data and privacy. We collect minimal data necessary for the app to function: information about running processes and their open files and sockets.

Data we do NOT collect

  • The app does not collect any analytical data, including anonymous analytics or crash logs.
  • The app does not collect any personal data.
  • No internet connection is required to use the app. Internet is only needed for auto updates, online process name search, and license key verification.
  • The app does not make any system changes without your explicit permission.
  • The app may request permissions in the following scenarios:
    • 'Show in Finder' feature is used from the main process table.
    • Opening the context menu in the Files tab.
    • Opening a process file using the 'Open with' context menu command.
    • Using the 'Find process with mouse click' feature.
  • We do not share any data with third-party services.

ProcessSpy Website Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is our policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our website, https://process-spy.app.

We do not collect any personal information, device information, or browser information.

Once the download button is clicked, we store the download date and time on our backend server.

This policy is effective as of 24 April 2024.
